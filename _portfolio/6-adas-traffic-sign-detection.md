**Feb. 2024 – Feb. 2025**

Deep learning for advanced driver assistance (ADAS), focused on traffic-sign
recognition in adverse weather.

- Built a two-stage pipeline pairing a **neural-network-based image restoration**
  module with a classifier.
- Achieved **25 dB PSNR** on restored images and about **80%** classification
  accuracy, indicating practical potential for real-world deployment.
