**Sept. 2023 – Sept. 2024**

An end-to-end system for monitoring and providing feedback on physical activity in
older adults.

- Built a pipeline with **YOLOv8-Pose**, detecting 17 keypoints per frame and
  converting joint angles into sequences for an **LSTM** classifier.
- Evaluated on 200 samples with a confusion matrix showing overall accuracy
  above **90%**.
